International Conference On Computational Intelligence & Engineering System Design

Call for Papers

CIESD-21 is soliciting original, and high-quality research papers addressing research challenges and advances in the tracks mentioned below. The maximum allowable length of the paper is 08 pages (including figures and references) for regular research articles strictly in SCOPUS SINGLE COLUMN FORMAT. Articles will be Double-Blind Peer-Reviewed by three experts to decide their suitability for publication in the conference. Acceptance of papers will be communicated to authors by email.

Researchers invited to speak, will present the latest development and technical solutions in the areas of: Computation Intelligence and Engineering System Design.

The theme of CIESD-21 is "Computational Intelligence & Engineering System Design". The topics of the conference includes the following reaserch areas, but are not limited to:
