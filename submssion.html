Submission

Conference Policies:

  1. All Selected and presented papers will be submitted for publication in Scopus Indexed Journal.
  2. At least one author of an accepted paper is required to register at the full registration rate. If an author has got more than one accepted papers, each paper has also to be registered.
  3. Receipt of registration will be provided during the conference.
  4. It may be ensured that the Remitter’s (Participant/Author) name, EasyChair Paper-ID and the purpose of remittance (Registration Fees) are clearly mentioned by the remitter in the funds transfer application. Indian Nationals or students residing overseas have to pay foreign author’s registration fee only.
  5. Student Member Registration: Students (UG/PG Students and only Full-time Research/PhD Scholars) have to submit their scanned copy of the ID card along with the registration form to avail of the deduction.
  6. Authors seeking official letter to apply for CONFERENCE GRANT from professional bodies may send their request at conference@kit.ac.in
  7. Submit Your paper by clicking the link: Submit Your Paper

Plagiarism Policies:

  1. CIESD-2021 strictly implements content plagiarism and self-plagiarism policy. Plagiarism found at any stage of the manuscript strict action will be taken against the authors.
  2. Plagiarism and Excessive Re-use of Author's Earlier Material: The contents of the manuscripts should be original and must not be submitted elsewhere simultaneously for consideration towards publication in another conference or journals. All submitted submissions will be checked by the Turnitin tool for plagiarism and self-plagiarism. Authors are also advised to use Turnitin to check the plagiarism in their manuscript.
  3. Similarity Index Explanation: The similarity score represents the "cumulative" sum of similarity scores with all documents in the CrossCheck database.
  4. Authors are advised to keep their similarity score well below ≤ 20%.

Note : All the manuscripts have a similarity index well below 20 will only be considered for review. Otherwise, the manuscript will be rejected without any reason and no notification will be sent to the authors regarding this.

Withdrawal Policy:

  1. Withdrawal of manuscript can be made at any time before its acceptance.
  2. Once the manuscript is accepted and acceptance mail has been sent to the authors After that it can’t be withdrawn and the same will be published in local conference proceedings.
  3. Authors willing to withdraw accepted manuscript need to discuss with conference chair.

Change of Author(s) & Affiliation(s) details: CIESD-2021 strictly restricts the authors not to change their details or sequence of authors. All the submitted authors name of the review manuscript must match with the final camera ready version of manuscript. In case, any difference or change in sequence is found at any stage of publication the manuscript will be considered as rejected and will be dropped from final proceedings.
